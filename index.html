Privacy Policy

Budgix — Smart Budget Tracker  ·  MahiDeveloper

Effective Date: June 2026

📋 Overview

Budgix ("the App") is developed and maintained by MahiDeveloper. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. By using Budgix, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

🚫 No Data Collection

Budgix does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data with us or any third party. The App operates entirely offline. Every piece of information you enter — including income amounts, expense records, categories, and notes — is stored exclusively on your own device.

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not access or collect your financial information
  • We do not track your location at any time
  • We do not use any analytics or crash reporting SDKs
  • We do not display any advertisements
  • We do not require an internet connection to function
  • We do not require account creation or login

📱 Local Storage Only

All data entered into Budgix is stored locally on your Android device using the Room persistence library (SQLite database). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or third party.

✓  If you uninstall the App, all locally stored transaction data is permanently and automatically deleted from your device.

⚙️ App Preferences

Budgix saves your theme preference (Light Mode or Dark Mode) using Android's SharedPreferences mechanism. This setting is stored locally on your device only and is never shared with or transmitted to any third party.

🔐 Data Security

Since all your data remains exclusively on your device, its security depends on your device's own security features — such as screen lock, PIN protection, or device encryption. We strongly recommend keeping your device secured. MahiDeveloper does not have access to your data at any time, under any circumstances.

🌐 Internet Permissions

Budgix does not request internet permission in its Android manifest. The App functions completely offline and does not make any network requests. Poppins fonts are loaded via Google Fonts provider on first use on devices with Google Play Services — this is handled by the OS, not the App, and no personal data is involved.

👶 Children's Privacy

Budgix is not directed at children under the age of 13. Because the App does not collect any personal information from any user whatsoever, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this App, please contact us — though this is technically not possible given our no-collection design.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the App evolves. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and notify users through the App's update release notes on Google Play. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
